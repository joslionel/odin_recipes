Beef Chili



Ingredients

  • Minced or diced beef
  • Beer
  • Plain flour
  • Beef stockc
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Onion
  • Two tins of black beans

Method


  1. Brown 5-800g of beef and and onion and 3 cloves of garlic
  2. Stir in a tablespoon of flour
  3. Add cumin, paprika and oregano to taste
  4. Add 100ml each of beef stock and beer
  5. Add 800g of tinned tomatoes
  6. Add two tins of black beans and include the liquid from one can
  7. Simmer until the beef is soft and tender - about 90 minutes
  8. Serve with rice or in a burrito
