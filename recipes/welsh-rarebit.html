Welsh Rarebit Recipe


Ingredients


  • Cheddar
  • Milk
  • Beer
  • Flour
  • Mustard
  • Garlic
  • Ham
  • Bread
  • Apple
  • Lemon juice

Method

  1. Make a roux with 50g butter & 50g flour
  2. Add 150ml milk and 150ml beer
  3. Stir until thick
  4. Add crushed garlic and mustard
  5. Add 250g grated cheddar and cook until a thick even consistency
  6. Spoon a small amount the cheese mixture onto slices of a toasted bread
  7. Use the mixture to stick a slice of ham to the bread
  8. Top with more of the cheese mixture
  9. Grill until golden brown
  10. Serve with apple sliced into matchsticks with lemon juice
